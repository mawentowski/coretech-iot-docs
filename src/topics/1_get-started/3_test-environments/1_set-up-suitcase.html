SITA Flex Suitcase

Learn how to set up a SITA Flex Suitcase for Application Development

Follow the steps in this section to verify the condition of your suitcase and that it is configured correctly to interact with Flex APIs.

Components

The suitcase is a self-contained Flex environment, and should consist of the following items:

  • Windows 10 Laptop
  • Boarding Pass Printer (Custom TK180)
  • Bagtag Printer (Custom TK180)
  • Travel Document Reader (DESKO IDenty Chrom)
  • Boarding Gate Reader (DESKO BGR 504 pro)

Verify Suitcase Condition

Hardware
  1. Check for visible signs of damage to the devices in the suitcase.
    • If there are, please inform SITA immediately at techsupport@techwritex.com before using the suitcase.
  2. Check if any damages are damaged.
    • If there are damaged devices, then disconnect those devices to ensure no further damage to them or the connected laptop.

Check and Power On Devices

Hardware
  1. Ensure that all devices are connected via their USB cables and power cables.
  2. Ensure that both the printers have enough stock loaded into them.
  3. Power on the devices and ensure the following occurs:
    • Printers - the screens light up with no errors on startup.
    • Travel Document Reader - a green light displays.
    • Boarding Gate Reader - the screen displays that it is ready.

Launch Windows 10 Desktop

Hardware
  1. Power on the Windows 10 Laptop.
  2. Ensure that it boots the Windows 10 Desktop.
    • The Peripheral View window displays on the laptop.
    • The Peripheral View runs locally on the workstation and displays the status of the devices.
    • If you do not see the Peripheral View as shown above, or no devices are showing under the VPS Devices tab, refer to the troubleshooting section Peripheral View Shows No devices.

Verify Devices Statuses

Hardware
  1. Ensure all the devices listed in the Peripheral View are showing as Online.
    • If you do not see Online statuses for all devices, refer to the troubleshooting Unknown Unreachable Device(s).
  2. Verify devices in other views are listed as Online.
    • If all statuses look correct, then your suitcase is ready for application development.
